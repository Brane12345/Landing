What is Locust Beans(IRU)

Locust bean gum, also called carob gum, is a natural thickner that's commonly added to packaged foods and has many uses in cooking and food manufacturing. In Nigeria, it is known as IRU especially to the yoruba tribe. It is of great importance to people in Nigeria as people rely on it as a source of food, traditional medicine and remedy for other ailments.

Benefits of Locust Bean(IRU)

  1. High in fiber
  2. Lowers blood sugar and blood fat levels
  3. Treatment of respiratory infections
